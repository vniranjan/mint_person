Direction 1 — Focused Single Column

Clean, centered, uncluttered. Summary cards above a chart above the transaction list — top-to-bottom reading flow. Information hierarchy is explicit: totals first, breakdown second, detail third. Generous whitespace creates calm. Best for users who want a clear answer before drilling in.

Direction 2 — Split Sidebar

Category breakdown always visible in the left sidebar as you scroll through transactions. Persistent context: you always know where you stand in each category without scrolling up. The sidebar doubles as a filter — click a category to show only those transactions. Dense but organized. Best for power users who review multiple categories per session.

Direction 3 — Editorial Minimal

Typographic-first, almost like a well-designed financial report. The category breakdown is a simple horizontal bar list — no chart widget, just data with proportion bars. Maximum whitespace, large heading, everything else subordinate. Feels premium and deliberate. Best for the "calm clarity" emotional goal — nothing competes for attention.
